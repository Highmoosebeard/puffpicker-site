What is Puff Picker?

Puff Picker is a cannabis-themed decision wheel for adults who already legally possess cannabis. Add your own strain names, spin the wheel, and let the app help pick your session vibe, snack, drink, activity, or style.

Important Disclaimer

Puff Picker does not sell cannabis, deliver cannabis, process cannabis purchases, direct users to dispensaries, or facilitate cannabis transactions.

Puff Picker is intended for adults age 21 and older in locations where cannabis possession and use are legal. Always follow your local laws. Do not drive or operate machinery while impaired.
